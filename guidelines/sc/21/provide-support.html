Comprehension support is available for:

Numerical content
Charts, tables, graphics or non-numerical text content are available that summarize numerical information;
Forms
Non-standard controls have instructions, and multi-step forms provide information about a user's position in the form;
Long blocks of text
Long blocks of content are divided with headings, or keywords are visually emphasized, or a summary is provided;
Directions
Alternative content is available for relative and cardinal directions.

Exceptions:

  • Content that must conform to a defined writing style, such as in an academic paper;
  • Structure is an essential part of the main function of the site, such as a game or a reading test;
  • Style is an essential part of the main function of the site, such as a literary work;
  • Numerical information where the target audience is in a profession that requires advanced knowledge of math;
  • Real numbers which do not require math operations or calculations to understand the meaning of the numbers.
